I see potential that people can't see yet. Then I build the conditions where they can see it for themselves.

## How I think

Everything I've built orbits a single rivalry: **Survival vs. Creation.**

Most people are running a survival operating system — not because they're weak, but because it's what worked. Fear, control, reaction, protection. It kept them alive. It also keeps them from building anything that actually matters to them.
